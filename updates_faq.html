Frequently Asked Questions

What is HydroLens?

HydroLens is an AI-powered water quality analysis tool designed to help users monitor and assess water conditions effectively.

How do I sign up or log in?

You can sign up or log in using your Google account via the OAuth authentication system integrated into the app.

Why is the MongoDB connection important?

MongoDB stores user data and chat history, enabling personalized experiences and data persistence.

What should I do if the app fails to load?

Ensure MongoDB is installed and running, and that your environment variables are correctly set, especially the MongoDB URI.

Where can I find the source code?

The source code is available on the project's GitHub repository linked in the README.
